Fun Facts

Famous American “Auto Train” enables passengers to sleep either in normal travel carriages

or to sleep in their cars that are loaded into special car freight wagons that consist of so-called “autoracks” modules.

The carrying capacity of this train is 320 cars.


Some of the most famous trains in the United States are owned by the Ringling Bros.

and Barnum & Bailey circus company that was founded in 1919.

Gear and manpower for their shows are carried by two very large trains called “Blue Tour” and the “Red Tour”.

Both of them have 40 passenger cars and 20 freight ones.
